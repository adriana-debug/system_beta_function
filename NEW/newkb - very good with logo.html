Work-from-Home Policy

Category: HR Policy | Author: HR Department | Version: 1.0 | Date: 11-Oct-2025

1. Purpose

This document defines the guidelines for employees authorized to work remotely, ensuring consistent productivity and adherence to company data security standards. The policy establishes an operational framework enabling flexible work arrangements while maintaining oversight and accountability.

2. Scope

The Work-from-Home Policy applies to all employees of Digital Minds BPO engaged in remote work. It excludes positions requiring physical presence at operational facilities.

3. Key Responsibilities

  • Employees are required to maintain a secure home environment suitable for work.
  • Supervisors must monitor task completion and performance metrics.
  • The HR Department is responsible for policy updates and compliance monitoring.

Work-from-Home Policy (cont.)

Version: 1.0 | Updated: 11-Oct-2025

4. Policy / Procedure

  1. Employees must complete a minimum of three months of service before eligibility for remote work.
  2. All remote work requests shall be submitted via the HR portal no later than two days before the intended start date.
  3. Employees are expected to be online from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, unless otherwise approved.
  4. Data access must occur through secure VPN connections, and company devices must not be shared with unauthorized users.

5. Communication Standards

Employees are expected to participate in daily check-ins with their managers. All communications shall be conducted using approved corporate channels such as Microsoft Teams and email.

6. IT and Security Provisions

  • VPN credentials must be safeguarded at all times.
  • All system logins are monitored to ensure compliance with information security policies.
  • Incidents or breaches must be reported to the IT Helpdesk immediately.
