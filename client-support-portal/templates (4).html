OPERATIONS-402 | Version 4.2 - Revised Jan 2025

High-Value Order Fulfillment

1. Scope of Document

This SOP outlines the mandatory verification steps for any order exceeding $2,500 USD. These orders require manual secondary review to mitigate fraud and ensure inventory accuracy prior to dispatch.

Checklist for Fulfillment

  • 01
    Cross-reference Shipping Address with Billing Data in Nexus-DB-1.
  • 02
    Photograph the physical items alongside the packing slip for the audit log.
  • 03
    Assign "Signature Required" status to the courier shipping manifest.

2. Escalation Paths

If the shipping address is a known freight forwarder or 3PL warehouse, the order must be escalated to the Risk Management Team via the internal ticketing system under category [FRAUD-QUERY].
