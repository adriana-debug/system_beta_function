NEW HIRE ORIENTATION (NHO) POLICY

1. Objective

The New Hire Orientation (NHO) program ensures that all new employees are properly introduced to Digital Minds BPO Services Inc., its culture, policies, expectations, and operational standards. In compliance with the Labor Code of the Philippines, which requires employers to inform employees of their duties, compensation, and workplace regulations, NHO serves as the official onboarding process for all new hires.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all newly hired employees, regardless of position, employment status, or department assignment. Attendance is mandatory.

3. Program Overview

During the New Hire Orientation, employees will:

  • Learn the company's history, mission, values, and culture.
  • Understand the organizational structure and meet key leaders.
  • Receive complete information on policies, rules, procedures, and company regulations.
  • Learn about compensation, benefits, statutory deductions, and government-mandated benefits (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG).
  • Be informed about workplace safety and data privacy regulations (in line with RA 10173 – Data Privacy Act).
  • Be provided with resources and support structures for successful integration.
4. Legal Compliance

This orientation ensures compliance with:

  • Labor Code of the Philippines – Employee rights, duties, and wage rules.
  • Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) – Handling of personal and client data.
  • DOLE Handbook on Workers’ Statutory Monetary Benefits – Mandatory contributions and entitlements.
  • Occupational Safety and Health Standards (RA 11058) – Safety briefing requirements.
5. Attendance Requirement

Attendance in NHO is strictly required. Failure to attend:

  • May result in the delay of onboarding activities, payroll enrollment, and system access.
  • May lead to the withdrawal of the employment offer if the absence is unjustified.
6. Responsibilities
  • HR Department: Facilitates the NHO, provides materials, and documents attendance.
  • New Hire: Must attend, participate, and comply with all onboarding instructions.
7. Effectivity

This policy is effective immediately upon release and applies to all incoming employees across all sites.
