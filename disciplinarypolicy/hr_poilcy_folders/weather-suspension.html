WORK SUSPENSION POLICY Digital Minds BPO

Guidelines on Work Suspension Due to Weather and Emergencies

1. Purpose

This policy provides guidelines for work suspension during typhoons, severe weather, and similar emergencies, ensuring employee safety and compliance with Philippine labor laws.

2. Scope

Applies to all employees of Digital Minds BPO.

3. Responsibilities
  • Site Point Persons (PM/OIC/EA) ensure the facility, equipment, and employees are safe and secure.
  • Management issues official announcements of work suspension based on LGU advisories and prevailing typhoon signals.
  • Site EAs report employees who report to work after suspension, hours worked, and attach LGU advisories.
4. Compensation

Employees affected by work suspension shall receive 30% of their basic rate as additional pay, compliant with Labor Code provisions on emergency situations.

5. Procedures
  1. Management monitors LGU advisories and weather conditions.
  2. Official suspension announcements are disseminated through company communication channels.
  3. Employees who work after suspension announcement log hours worked for proper compensation.
  4. HR documents attendance and approves additional hazard pay.
6. Compliance with Philippine Labor Laws
  • Work suspension and hazard pay policies comply with the Labor Code.
  • Employees cannot be penalized for following official suspension announcements.
  • Pay for hours worked during suspension is computed and reflected in payroll accurately.
