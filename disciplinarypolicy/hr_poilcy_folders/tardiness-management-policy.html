TARDINESS MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. Objective

This policy establishes clear guidelines for managing tardiness to promote punctuality, improve operational efficiency, and ensure fairness. Deductions and disciplinary actions described herein are compliant with the Labor Code of the Philippines, including rules on wage deductions (Art. 113), working hours (Art. 82–90), and disciplinary due process.

2. Coverage

This policy applies to all employees regardless of rank, position, or employment status.

3. Definition

Tardiness refers to arriving for work after the scheduled start time, regardless of the number of minutes late.

4. Policy on Deductions

Accumulated tardiness per cut-off period will result in the following deductions:

Tardiness Duration (per cut-off) Deduction
Less than 30 minutes Hourly rate deduction
30 minutes to 4 hours Half-day rate deduction
More than 4 hours Full-day rate deduction

Legal Basis: Deductions are allowed under Labor Code Art. 113 as long as they are: (a) for the employee's benefit, (b) authorized by law, or (c) authorized by a company policy known to the employee.

5. Perfect Attendance Eligibility

Any tardiness, regardless of duration, disqualifies an employee from perfect attendance incentives (if applicable).

6. Enforcement

Operations Admins and Team Leads must ensure consistent enforcement of this policy across all teams.

7. Performance Management
  • Recurring tardiness is subject to disciplinary action following the Twin Notice Rule.
  • Three (3) counts of tardiness = One (1) absence. (Counts reset every 6 months.)
  • Severe lateness may be considered Habitual Neglect of Duty (Labor Code Art. 297).
8. Procedure
  1. Team Leaders record tardiness in the official attendance tracker.
  2. Team Leader notifies HR when tardiness or absences exceed allowable limits.
  3. HR reviews the attendance record and initiates disciplinary action if necessary, ensuring compliance with due process.
9. Effectivity

This policy takes effect immediately and supersedes previous tardiness guidelines.
