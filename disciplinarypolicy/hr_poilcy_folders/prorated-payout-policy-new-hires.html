PRORATED PAYOUT POLICY FOR NEW HIRES

1. Objective

This policy outlines the guidelines for prorated payouts for new hires who begin employment in the middle of a payroll cut-off period.

2. Policy Statement

Digital Minds BPO Services Inc. is committed to ensuring fair and accurate payroll practices. When a new hire starts on the 2nd day or later within any cut-off period, their payout for that cut-off may be held and released on the following pay period to maintain payroll accuracy and consistency.

3. Cut-Off Periods
  • 1st Cut-off: 1st to 15th of the month
  • 2nd Cut-off: 16th to the last day of the month
4. Payout Rules
  • If a new hire starts on the 1st day of the cut-off, payout for that period is released as scheduled.
  • If a new hire starts on the 2nd day or after of the cut-off, payout for that cut-off period is withheld and released together with the next payroll cycle.
5. Release of Withheld Payout

Withheld payouts are released in the next scheduled payroll cut-off to ensure accurate timekeeping, attendance validation, and payroll computation.

Example:
If an employee starts on January 2 (during the 1st cut-off), their payout for January 1–15 will be withheld and released during the next payroll released on January 30.

6. Effective Date

This policy takes effect on January 1, 2026, unless revised or superseded by a new version.
