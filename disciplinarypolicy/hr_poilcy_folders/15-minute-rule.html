HOUSE RULES Digital Minds BPO

HOUSE RULES POLICY

1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide clear guidance on expected behavior, use of company property, and operational discipline within Digital Minds BPO premises. These rules ensure safety, professionalism, and a productive work environment while protecting company assets and confidential information.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, visitors, and other personnel entering or working within Digital Minds BPO premises, including production and administrative areas.

3. General House Rules
  1. Bags are not allowed inside the production area.
  2. No gadgets (phones, tablets, laptops, etc.) are allowed inside the production floor unless specifically authorized.
  3. Flash drives, external hard drives, and other removable storage devices are prohibited in production areas.
  4. Noysi shall be used for internal team communication only. Any use of obscene, vulgar, or offensive language is strictly prohibited.
  5. Use of proxy websites or VPN inside production areas is prohibited.
  6. Watching videos, listening to music, or saving media on workstations (e.g., YouTube) is prohibited.
  7. Administrative stations may only be accessed with explicit permission.
  8. No food is allowed inside the production floor; only bottled water or spill-proof containers are permitted.
  9. Practice CLAYGO (Clean-As-You-Go) in all areas; proper waste segregation and disposal is required.
  10. After shifts, employees must shut down units, tuck chairs, and properly store headsets.
  11. Sleeping during scheduled shifts on the production floor is strictly prohibited.
  12. Employees must always wear their ID while on the premises.
  13. Access to air conditioning units is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  14. Maintain respect for colleagues at all times; discriminatory or derogatory behavior is prohibited.
  15. Minimize noise and avoid disrupting othersâ€™ work, especially during calls or meetings.
  16. Company property and equipment must be used responsibly; damages or malfunctions must be reported immediately.
  17. Harassment, bullying, or physical violence towards colleagues is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate termination.
  18. Alcohol consumption and gambling activities are forbidden on company premises.
  19. Loud music or behavior that disturbs others is not permitted.
  20. Maintain confidentiality of sensitive company, client, and employee information as per NDAs and company agreements.
  21. Within production areas, only indoor-use slippers are allowed; outdoor slippers are strictly prohibited.
  22. The elevator is restricted for administrative personnel and PWDs only.
  23. All employees must comply with the Dress Code Policy:
    • Monday: Smart Casual
    • Tuesday to Thursday: Casual
    • Friday to Sunday: Dress Down
4. Compliance and Enforcement

All employees are required to adhere to these rules. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, including verbal or written warnings, suspension, or termination, consistent with Philippine Labor Code provisions.

5. Interpretation

For any ambiguity in these rules, HR and management will interpret and apply them in a fair, consistent, and legally compliant manner. Any exceptions or amendments require formal approval.
