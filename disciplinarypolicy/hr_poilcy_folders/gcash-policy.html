GCash Transaction & Mastercard Policy

1. GCash Transaction Reversal Request

Authorized POC: Alexis Joyce G. Fausto, HR Officer

  • Mobile: 0956-490-1006
  • Email: alexis.f@dmibpo.com
2. Company Wallet Details
  • Company Name: Digital Minds BPO Services Inc.
  • GCash Wallet Account: dm-bpo-p3
3. Required Documents from Employee
  1. Completed and signed Reversal Letter
  2. Valid government-issued ID
  3. Three (3) specimen signatures
4. Process Flow
  1. Document preparation by employee: Complete reversal letter, provide ID and specimen signatures, submit to HR.
  2. HR Verification & submission: Verify documents, log in tracker, email partnersupport@gcash.com with CC to COO.
  3. Follow-up: Monitor email for ticket number and updates; communicate to concerned employee.
  4. Disbursement: HR submits disbursement request; COO approves; funds released to HR Officer.
5. Processing Timeline
  • GCash Support Team Initial Response: 1–2 business days
  • Resolution Time: 1–2 business days after confirmation
6. Important Notes
  • HR Officer is the primary liaison.
  • Incomplete documents will not be processed.
  • Strict compliance with GCash partner support guidelines.
  • Maintain confidentiality of all transaction details.
7. GCash Mastercard Policy
  • Issued to new hires upon completion of training.
  • Used exclusively for payroll.
  • Free for employees with ≥6 months service; Php150 fee for less than 6 months.
  • Lost card replacement: Php150 fee, report immediately to HR.
  • Employees are responsible for card security; unauthorized use must be reported.
